TigAR: an AR Campus Map

Created an augmented reality campus map application for mobile smart phone devices. This application is used to help locate specific buildings on the Pacific campus and shows information about the building. The three core functionalities of TigAR are:
- Scanning specific images correlated to certain buildings that will display information about it on the screen
- A GPS guidance that overlays targets in the world (augmented reality) that shows users where to go
- Traditional 2D map with zooming capabilities
The audience for TigAR are lost and confused people on the UOP campus.
All of the information used (such as building information) are taken from the University of the Pacific official website.
